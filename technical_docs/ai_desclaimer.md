## A note on AI usage in A-Life project

This is a lerning project. I intend to learn new concepts from computation and biology by merging these two fields together. The result of this investigations are Notes and Codes. Notes are either thesis, anti-thesis or synthesis (by me most of the times unless mentioned). Codes are my attempts to turn biological concepts, entities, processes and mechanisms into a computation setting. There will be also formal explanations (within the informal Notes) from logic to mathematics of the underlying computational processes of biological mechanisms and dynamics. 

I have to admit that I've extensively used Artificial Intelligence (LLMs) in this project for both Codes part and Notes part. But I will flag the AI generated result with something like: `AI-GENERATED` so the boundry between what is original in the project and what is AI generated to be clearer. I still have to say that I'm a technology optimist and I believe AI can empower us in lot's of - still not recognized - ways. 

As I said before, the main objective of this project is to learn constructively new ideas ranging from theory and practice in an interdiciplinary setting. So I allow myself to use AI for that end. To undrestand. To satisfy this essential purpose I will mention tips that one can follow to grasp more from AI not a result based manner but in expantion of undrestanding. 

Stellarous.
